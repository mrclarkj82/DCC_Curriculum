# Rubric: Broadcast Desk Update Response

## 4 Points

Complete, polished, on time, and clearly meets or extends the stated requirements.

## 3 Points

Complete and meets the stated requirements.

## 2 Points

Partially complete or missing required evidence.

## 1 Points

Attempted but incomplete, unclear, or not functional.

## 0 Points

Not submitted.
