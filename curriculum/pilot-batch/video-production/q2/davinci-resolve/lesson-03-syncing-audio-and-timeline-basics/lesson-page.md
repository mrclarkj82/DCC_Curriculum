## Vocabulary

- Audio sync: The process of matching separate audio and video so dialogue or sound lines up correctly.
- Waveform: A visual shape that shows the loud and quiet parts of audio.
- Edit page: The Resolve page used to build and refine a story in a timeline.
- Source viewer: The viewer used to preview a clip before adding it to the timeline.
- Timeline viewer: The viewer that shows the edited sequence at the playhead.
- Playhead: The marker that shows the current time position in a clip or timeline.
- Track: A horizontal lane in the timeline that holds video or audio clips.

## Common Problems and Fixes

- Students may confuse previewing a clip with editing it into the timeline.
- Students may not notice whether they are watching the source viewer or timeline viewer.
- Students may ignore waveforms even when audio gives useful editing clues.
