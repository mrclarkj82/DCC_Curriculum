## Vocabulary

- Checkpoint quiz: A short assessment used to check core vocabulary and workflow understanding.
- Audio level: The loudness of a clip or track.
- Fade: A gradual change into or out of video or audio.
- Inspector: The panel used to adjust properties of a selected clip, title, effect, or audio item.
- Linked clips: Video and audio that stay selected or moved together unless unlinked.
- Cleanup pass: A focused editing pass that removes obvious problems before adding polish.
