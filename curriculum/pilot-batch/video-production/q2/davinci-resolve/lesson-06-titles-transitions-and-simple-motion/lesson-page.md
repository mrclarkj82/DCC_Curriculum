## Vocabulary

- Effects panel: The panel that contains transitions, titles, generators, and other edit-page effects.
- Transition: A visual change between two clips, such as a dissolve or wipe.
- Title: Generated text placed over or between video clips.
- Lower third: A title placed in the lower part of the screen, often used for names or labels.
- Generator: A generated visual element such as a color or gradient.
- Keyframe: A point in time that stores a value so an effect or property can change over time.
- Inspector: The panel used to adjust selected titles, clips, transitions, and effects.

## Common Problems and Fixes

- Students may use flashy transitions instead of motivated cuts.
- Students may make titles too small, too fast, or low contrast.
- Students may add motion without checking if it improves communication.
