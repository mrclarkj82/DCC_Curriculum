# Dialogue, Music, and Sound Pass Assignment Sheet

## Objective

I can organize audio tracks and make a basic sound pass that balances dialogue, music, ambience, and effects.

## Skill Focus

- track organization
- audio balancing
- Fairlight navigation
- sound layering
- basic EQ awareness

## Required Steps

1. Open the rough cut or teacher-provided audio practice timeline.
2. Switch to the Fairlight page and identify the timeline, mixer, meters, mute, and solo controls.
3. Name or color at least three audio tracks or layers.
4. Balance dialogue or main sound so it is easy to hear.
5. Add or adjust one ambience, music, or sound effect layer.
6. Capture evidence of the Fairlight timeline or mixer and explain the mix choice.

## Naming Convention

Use this format when naming screenshots, exported evidence, or written files: LastName_FirstName_VP-Q2-A06_Description.

## Google Drive / YouTube Link Evidence Requirements

Upload screenshots, short screen recordings, exported review files, or written evidence to Google Drive, Google Docs, or YouTube according to teacher directions. Paste share links into DCC Creative Studio. Do not upload raw video files directly to the website.

- Google Drive link to a screenshot of organized Fairlight tracks or mixer.
- Google Drive or YouTube link to an exported audio-improved rough cut if teacher requests export.
- Short reflection explaining one audio problem and how you fixed it.

## What To Do If Stuck

- Check that the correct Resolve project is open.
- Use the correct Resolve page for the task before changing random settings.
- Ask whether the issue is media organization, timeline editing, audio, title, color, or export.
- Capture evidence from the actual Resolve workspace, not only the desktop.

## 4-Point Rubric

- 4: Complete, polished, on time, and clearly meets or extends the stated requirements.
- 3: Complete and meets the stated requirements.
- 2: Partially complete or missing required evidence.
- 1: Attempted but incomplete, unclear, or not functional.
- 0: Not submitted.

## Extension Challenge

Route similar sound effects through a bus and apply a shared volume or EQ adjustment.

## Studio Challenge

Create a sound-design pass that makes the same rough cut feel calm, tense, or energetic.

## Reflection Prompt

What audio change made the biggest difference for the audience?
