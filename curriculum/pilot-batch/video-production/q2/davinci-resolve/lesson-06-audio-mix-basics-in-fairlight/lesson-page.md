## Vocabulary

- Fairlight: The Resolve page designed for audio editing, mixing, and sound design.
- Mixer: The panel used to adjust track volume, pan, EQ, effects, and routing.
- Meter: A visual display showing audio level during playback.
- Mute: A control that turns a track off.
- Solo: A control that plays one track while temporarily muting others.
- Ambience: Background room tone or environmental sound that makes a scene feel present.
- Bus: A routing path that groups tracks into a shared mix or effects chain.
- EQ: Equalization, used to adjust frequency ranges in sound.
