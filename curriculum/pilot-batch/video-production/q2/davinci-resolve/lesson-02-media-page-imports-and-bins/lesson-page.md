## Vocabulary

- Media page: The Resolve workspace focused on finding, previewing, importing, and organizing media.
- Media Storage: The panel used to browse files on the computer or connected drives.
- Media Pool: The project area that contains clips, audio, timelines, and bins available to the edit.
- Bin: A folder inside the Resolve project used to organize media.
- Metadata: Information about a clip, such as shot, scene, take, comments, or technical details.
- Smart bin: A bin that gathers clips automatically based on rules or metadata filters.

## Common Problems and Fixes

- Students may think viewing footage in Media Storage means it has been imported.
- Students may drag folders into the wrong panel and lose folder organization.
- Students may treat bins as decoration instead of a workflow tool.

## Student-Facing Help

- Save the Resolve project before switching pages or exporting.
- Keep evidence links in Google Drive, Google Docs, or YouTube according to teacher directions.
- Use the class naming convention for screenshots, exports, and written evidence.
- Do not upload raw files directly to the website.
