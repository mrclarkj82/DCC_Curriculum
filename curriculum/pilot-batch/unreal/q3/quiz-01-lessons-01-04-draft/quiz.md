## 1. What is the main purpose of a production log during the Q3 castle project?

- Question ID: ue-q3-quiz-01-q01
- Type: multiple-choice
- Related lesson ID: ue-q3-l01
- Difficulty: easy
- Tag: production-log

- To track what was built, captured, changed, and planned next.
- To replace all screenshots.
- To store secret passwords.

Explanation: A production log records work and decisions so the documentary has accurate process evidence.

## 7. A student has a strong final screenshot but no process clips from the day. What should they do next class?

- Question ID: ue-q3-quiz-01-q07
- Type: scenario
- Related lesson ID: ue-q3-l01

## 8. A castle looks impressive from editor view but too small when the player walks through it. What should the student check?

- Type: scenario
- Related lesson ID: ue-q3-l03
- Difficulty: medium
- Tag: troubleshooting

Correct answer: They should test from player view and adjust scale, doors, paths, walls, or camera framing based on the character size.

Explanation: Player-view testing reveals scale problems that editor view can hide.

## 9. Put the daily Q3 evidence workflow in order: build, record, log, upload, submit.

- Question ID: ue-q3-quiz-01-q09
- Type: process-order
- Related lesson ID: ue-q3-l01
- Difficulty: medium
- Tag: workflow

Correct answer: 1. Build or revise the castle. 2. Record screen/camera evidence. 3. Update the production log. 4. Upload evidence to Drive or YouTube. 5. Submit the link in DCC Creative Studio.

Explanation: This sequence keeps production work and evidence connected.

## 10. In 2-3 sentences, explain why Q3 requires daily recording while students build the castle.

- Question ID: ue-q3-quiz-01-q10
- Type: short-response
- Related lesson ID: ue-q3-l01
- Difficulty: hard
- Tag: short-response

Correct answer: A strong answer explains that daily recording captures the process, decisions, mistakes, revisions, and progress that a final screenshot cannot show. It should connect the evidence to the final making-of documentary.

Explanation: This checks whether students understand the documentary purpose of daily evidence.
