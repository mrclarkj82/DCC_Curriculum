## Student Workflow

1. Open the Q3 castle project and confirm the lesson target: I can review early Q3 production expectations and refine the castle landscape while documenting visible progress.
2. Complete or review Q3 Quiz 1 when assigned.
3. Choose one landscape problem to improve.
4. Refine terrain shape, scale, or route readability.
5. Capture before/after screenshots or a short screen recording of the refinement.
6. Update the production log with the improvement and remaining issue.
7. Capture daily screen recording or camera footage that shows the process.
8. Upload evidence to Google Drive or YouTube and set sharing so the teacher can view it.
9. Submit the approved evidence link in DCC Creative Studio with a short reflection.
