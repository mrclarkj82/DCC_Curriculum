## Student Workflow

1. Open the Q3 castle project and confirm the lesson target: I can review scene-building concepts and improve lighting mood while documenting how lighting changes the castle environment.
2. Complete or review Q3 Quiz 3 when assigned.
3. Choose a lighting mood for the castle environment.
4. Adjust lighting direction, intensity, color, or time-of-day feel.
5. Capture before/after evidence from the same view.
6. Update the production log with the lighting mood decision.
7. Capture daily screen recording or camera footage that shows the process.
8. Upload evidence to Google Drive or YouTube and set sharing so the teacher can view it.
9. Submit the approved evidence link in DCC Creative Studio with a short reflection.
