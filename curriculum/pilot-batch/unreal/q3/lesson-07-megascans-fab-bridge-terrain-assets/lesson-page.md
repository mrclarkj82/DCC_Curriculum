## Student Workflow

1. Open the Q3 castle project and confirm the lesson target: I can import and place terrain assets responsibly while documenting how assets support the castle environment.
2. Import or choose terrain assets appropriate for the castle environment.
3. Place assets to support the route, silhouette, or story.
4. Adjust scale, rotation, and placement so assets feel intentional.
5. Record a clip showing one asset before and after placement.
6. Update the production log with why the asset was chosen.
7. Capture daily screen recording or camera footage that shows the process.
8. Upload evidence to Google Drive or YouTube and set sharing so the teacher can view it.
9. Submit the approved evidence link in DCC Creative Studio with a short reflection.
