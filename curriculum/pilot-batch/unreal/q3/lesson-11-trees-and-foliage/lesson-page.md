## Student Workflow

1. Open the Q3 castle project and confirm the lesson target: I can place trees and foliage intentionally while documenting how natural details support the castle environment.
2. Add or refine trees, grass, or foliage around the castle environment.
3. Use density and placement to support story, route, or scale.
4. Avoid blocking important views or player movement.
5. Record a clip showing one foliage area before and after improvement.
6. Update the production log with what the foliage adds to the scene.
7. Capture daily screen recording or camera footage that shows the process.
8. Upload evidence to Google Drive or YouTube and set sharing so the teacher can view it.
9. Submit the approved evidence link in DCC Creative Studio with a short reflection.
