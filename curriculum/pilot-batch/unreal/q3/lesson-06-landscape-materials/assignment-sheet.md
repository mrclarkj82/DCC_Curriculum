# Landscape Material Test Assignment Sheet

## Objective

I can apply and evaluate landscape materials while documenting how material choices change the castle environment mood.

## Skill Focus

- landscape materials
- surface readability
- mood
- screen recording
- production log

## Required Steps

1. Apply or adjust at least two landscape material areas.
2. Compare how the material choices affect the castle mood and readability.
3. Capture screenshots from the same camera angle before and after material changes.
4. Record a short clip explaining one material choice.
5. Update the production log with the material decision and next improvement.

## File / Project Naming Convention

Use this format when naming project folders, screenshots, recordings, production logs, and written evidence: LastName_FirstName_UE-Q3-A06_Description.

## Evidence Requirements

- Before/after screenshots of landscape material changes.
- Daily documentary clip explaining one material choice.
- Production log entry for today.

## Google Drive Link Submission Requirements

- Upload screenshots, screen recordings, camera clips, production log notes, or documentary draft evidence to Google Drive or YouTube according to teacher directions.
- Set link sharing so the teacher can view the evidence.
- Paste the evidence link into DCC Creative Studio.
- Do not upload raw media files directly to the website.
- If the evidence is a folder, make sure every required file inside the folder is viewable.

## What To Do If Stuck

- Re-read the learning target and identify the one visible skill you need to prove today.
- Capture a short before clip before making large changes.
- Ask whether the issue is project organization, camera view, scale, terrain, materials, assets, lighting, or evidence capture.
- Use screenshots plus production notes if screen recording fails.
- Keep working on the castle project while documenting the decision you can explain.

## Four-Point Rubric

- 4: Complete, polished landscape material test; daily documentary evidence is clear, organized, and strongly explains process decisions.
- 3: Complete landscape material test; daily documentary evidence and production log are present.
- 2: Partially complete landscape material test; documentary evidence or production log is incomplete or unclear.
- 1: Attempted but incomplete; little process evidence is visible.
- 0: Not submitted.

## Extension Challenge

Create a small material test zone with three labeled surface options.

## Studio Challenge

Add one optional behind-the-scenes clip or caption that could help the final documentary explain today's build decision.

## Reflection Prompt

Which material choice best supports your castle environment mood?
