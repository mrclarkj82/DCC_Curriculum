# Bell Ringer and Exit Ticket: Player Walkthrough

## Bell-Ringer Prompt

What should a viewer understand after watching a walkthrough of your castle?

## Response Type

Short response in DCC Creative Studio.

## Teacher Look-Fors

- Student connects the response to the castle environment project.
- Student mentions process documentation, screen recording, camera footage, or production log evidence when appropriate.
- Student can name a concrete choice instead of giving a vague answer.

## Likely Misconceptions

- Submitting only final screenshots with no process evidence.
- Recording too late, after the decision-making is already invisible.
- Forgetting to update the production log or Drive sharing permissions.

## Exit-Ticket Prompt

What part of the walkthrough needs improvement before final submission?

## Teacher Checkoff Guidance

- Complete: response names a specific build choice and a specific piece of documentary evidence.
- Partial: response names the build choice but not the evidence, or names evidence without explaining the choice.
- Needs follow-up: response is missing, too vague, or shows confusion about the lesson target.

## Follow-Up Questions

- What did you capture today that a final screenshot would not show?
- Which part of today's evidence belongs in the final making-of documentary?
- What should you record first next class so the process remains visible?

## Assignment Connection

Today's exit ticket should help students finish the reflection for Castle Player Walkthrough Recording.
