## 1. What is scene framing supposed to do?

- Question ID: ue-q3-quiz-03-q01
- Type: multiple-choice
- Related lesson ID: ue-q3-l10
- Difficulty: easy
- Tag: framing

Choices:
- Guide the viewer toward important areas.
- Hide all evidence.
- Delete the player path.
- Replace lighting.

Correct answer: Guide the viewer toward important areas.

Explanation: Framing uses foreground, middle ground, and background to direct attention.

## 7. A student paints dense trees directly over the player path. What should they adjust?

- Question ID: ue-q3-quiz-03-q07
- Type: scenario
- Related lesson ID: ue-q3-l11
- Difficulty: medium
- Tag: troubleshooting

Correct answer: They should reduce density, clear the path, group foliage intentionally, and record before/after readability evidence.

Explanation: Foliage should support the route and mood, not hide navigation.

- Related lesson ID: ue-q3-l12

## 9. Put the lighting mood workflow in order: choose mood word, capture before, adjust lighting, capture after, explain the change.

- Type: process-order
- Related lesson ID: ue-q3-l13
