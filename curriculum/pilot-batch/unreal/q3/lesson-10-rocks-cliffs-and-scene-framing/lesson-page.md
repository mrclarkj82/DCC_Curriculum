## Student Workflow

1. Open the Q3 castle project and confirm the lesson target: I can use rocks, cliffs, or terrain props to frame the castle environment and document how framing guides the viewer.
2. Add or refine rocks, cliffs, or terrain features around the castle.
3. Use placement to frame the route, entrance, or focal point.
4. Test at least one player-view or camera-view angle.
5. Record a clip showing how framing changed the scene.
6. Update the production log with the framing choice.
7. Capture daily screen recording or camera footage that shows the process.
8. Upload evidence to Google Drive or YouTube and set sharing so the teacher can view it.
9. Submit the approved evidence link in DCC Creative Studio with a short reflection.
