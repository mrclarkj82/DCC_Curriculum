# Sculpted Terrain Study Assignment Sheet

## Objective

I can create, sculpt, and shape a basic Unreal landscape with intentional terrain forms.

## Skill Focus

- landscape creation
- terrain sculpting
- smooth and flatten tools
- elevation zones
- scale-aware screenshot evidence

## Required Steps

1. Create or open a clean Unreal map for the terrain study.
2. Enter Landscape mode and create a basic landscape.
3. Sculpt at least three elevation zones such as low area, hill, ridge, valley, or platform.
4. Use Smooth to reduce harsh artifacts or unrealistic bumps.
5. Use Flatten to create one intentional playable or focal area.
6. Check the scene from a wide camera angle and a closer terrain angle.
7. Upload evidence screenshots to Google Drive and paste links into DCC Creative Studio.
8. Write a reflection naming the landscape tools you used.

## Naming Convention

Use this format when naming screenshots or exported evidence: LastName_FirstName_UE-Q1-A15_Description.

## Google Drive Link Evidence Requirements

Upload screenshots, short screen recordings, or written evidence to Google Drive. Paste share links into DCC Creative Studio. Do not upload raw files directly to the website.

- Google Drive link to a screenshot of Landscape mode or landscape setup.
- Google Drive link to a wide shot of sculpted terrain.
- Google Drive link to a close-up showing terrain shaping or refinement.
- Short reflection naming the landscape tools used.

## What To Do If Stuck

- Start broad first; use large brush sizes for big forms before small details.
- Use Shift with sculpting when you need to lower terrain.
- Use Smooth after extreme sculpting to remove harsh artifacts.
- Use Flatten to create a level area for a player path, structure, or focal object.

## 4-Point Rubric

- 4: Complete, polished, on time, and clearly meets or extends the stated requirements.
- 3: Complete and meets the stated requirements.
- 2: Partially complete or missing required evidence.
- 1: Attempted but incomplete, unclear, or not functional.
- 0: Not submitted.

## Extension Challenge

Add a clear path, overlook, valley, or buildable platform that could support a future mini-world.

## Studio Challenge

Use terrain shape to guide the viewer toward one focal point without adding signs or labels.

## Reflection Prompt

Name at least three landscape tools or settings you used and explain what each one changed.
