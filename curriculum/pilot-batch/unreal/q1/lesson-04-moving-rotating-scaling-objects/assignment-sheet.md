# Assignment Sheet: Starter Scene Transform Challenge

## Objective

I can build a simple starter scene by moving, rotating, scaling, duplicating, and placing objects from the toolbar and Content Drawer.

## Skill Focus

- translation
- rotation
- scale
- snapping
- duplication
- scene composition

## Required Steps

1. Use starter content objects or simple shapes to build a small scene.
2. Move at least three objects with the W shortcut or move gizmo.
3. Rotate at least two objects with the E shortcut or rotate gizmo.
4. Scale at least one object with the R shortcut or scale gizmo.
5. Duplicate at least one object using Ctrl+D or Alt-drag.
6. Capture a screenshot of the completed simple scene and write a short reflection naming at least three tools or shortcuts used.

## Naming Convention

Use the class naming convention your teacher assigns. Recommended starter pattern: `LastName_FirstName_UE-Q1-A04`.

## Screenshot/File Evidence Requirements

- Screenshot of completed simple scene
- Short reflection naming at least three tools or shortcuts used

## What To Do If Stuck

- Rewatch the assigned video segment: 00:17:31-00:27:14.
- Check the Common Problems and Fixes section in the lesson page.
- Ask a classmate to name the tool or panel you are trying to use.
- If the project or interface is broken, take a screenshot before asking for help.

## 4-Point Rubric

- **4:** Complete, polished, on time, and clearly meets or extends the stated requirements.
- **3:** Complete and meets the stated requirements.

- **0:** Not submitted.

## Extension Challenge

Use snapping intentionally to align objects and add one note explaining where snapping helped your scene.

## Reflection Prompt

Name three tools or shortcuts you used, and explain which one helped your scene most.
