- Concise bullet content: Material: An Unreal asset that defines how a surface looks when rendered.; Material graph: The node-based workspace used to build and edit a material.; Node: A block in the graph that stores or processes information before sending it to a material input.; Base color: The main visible color of a material before lighting effects are applied.; Roughness: A value that controls how sharp or blurry reflections appear on a surface.; Metallic: A value that controls whether a surface behaves like metal or a non-metal.
