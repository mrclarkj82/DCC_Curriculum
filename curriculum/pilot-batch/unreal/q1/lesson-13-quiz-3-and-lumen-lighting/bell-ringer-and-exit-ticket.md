# Quiz 3 and Lumen Lighting Bell Ringer and Exit Ticket

## Bell Ringer Prompt

What makes lighting in a 3D scene feel realistic instead of flat?

## Expected Student Response Type

Short written response in 2-4 sentences. Students should use specific Unreal vocabulary when possible.

## Teacher Look-For Notes

- Students connect the prompt to a visible Unreal workflow, scene choice, or setting.
- Students name what they know and what they still need to test.
- Misconceptions from the bell ringer can become quick demo points before independent work.

## Exit Ticket Prompt

What is one thing Lumen helps simulate, and how can you prove it with evidence?

## Exit Ticket Grading/Checkoff Guidance

- Complete: names a specific change, setting, or skill and explains it clearly.
- Developing: names a general activity but does not connect it to the lesson target.
- Needs follow-up: response is missing, too vague, or shows confusion about the lesson target.

## Possible Follow-Up Questions

- What evidence would prove that light is bouncing?
- When is a dark shadow useful, and when does it hide the scene?
- How could a creator use colored bounce light to tell a story?
